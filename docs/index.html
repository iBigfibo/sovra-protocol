The Problem

Emerging market sovereign bonds offer yields of 8 to 10% in US dollars, far above US Treasuries (4%). Yet these yields are locked behind high walls: minimum investments of $100k or more, complex broker accounts, physical presence requirements, KYC and tax paperwork, and illiquid secondary markets. Crypto users and retail investors are completely excluded. At the same time, RWA protocols onchain focus almost exclusively on low yield US T bills (3–5%), leaving a massive gap in permissionless high yield fixed income.

The Solution

Sovra tokenizes real emerging market Eurobonds into svTokens, each representing a $1 claim on a diversified portfolio of sovereign bonds. Users deposit USDC, and 90% of the deposit is used by a regulated partner to purchase actual bonds. The remaining 10% stays onchain as a liquidity reserve. Bond coupons are collected, converted to USDC, and distributed to svToken holders after deducting transparent fees. No KYC, no lock up, full DeFi composability.

Key benefit – You earn real bond yield, not token inflation or risky lending interest. The yield exists regardless of market conditions.

How It Works (Step by Step)

1

Connect Wallet

Any EVM wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, etc.). No sign up, no KYC, no email.

2

Deposit USDC

Enter amount. A 0.1% deposit fee is deducted. 90% of the net deposit is deployed to buy real sovereign Eurobonds via a regulated custodian. 10% stays onchain as instant redemption reserve.

3

Receive svTokens

You receive svNIGERIA (or other country tokens) at 1:1 rate with the net deposited USDC. These tokens represent your share of the bond portfolio.

4

Yield Accrual

Bonds pay semi-annual coupons. The custodian forwards the USDC coupon to the Sovra smart contract, which distributes it pro rata to all svToken holders. You can choose monthly, quarterly, half yearly or annual payouts. No action required – yield is automatic.

5

Redeem Anytime

Withdraw by burning your svTokens. Small redemptions come from the 10% onchain reserve instantly (0.1% withdrawal fee). Larger redemptions trigger a bond sale (1–2 days). No lock ups, no penalties for normal use.

Why Sovra Is the Best

Compared to DeFi lending (Aave, Compound) and other RWA protocols (Ondo, OpenEden), Sovra offers unique advantages:

  • Higher yield – 7–10% net APY vs 4–5% from US Treasuries or 4–7% from variable lending.
  • Real sovereign backing – Your yield comes from government bonds, not unsecured loans or protocol incentives.
  • No KYC, permissionless – Unlike Ondo or OpenEden, you only need a wallet. No whitelist, no accredited investor checks.
  • Instant liquidity for small exits – 10% onchain reserve ensures you can withdraw without waiting for bond settlement.
  • Global diversification – Six countries and growing, reducing single country default risk.
  • Transparent proof of reserves – Daily custodian attestation and monthly independent audits. The bond ISINs are public.

Competitive edge – Sovra is the first protocol to bring permissionless high yield EM sovereign bonds onchain. No competitor offers 7–10% USD yield from real government debt without KYC and lock ups.

Fee Structure

We keep fees minimal and transparent:

  • Deposit fee: 0.1% of deposited USDC
  • Withdrawal fee: 0.1% of withdrawn USDC (when redeeming svTokens)
  • Yield fee: 6.57% of gross bond yield (this covers protocol operations, custodian fees, and development). Net APY to users is 8.41% for the Nigeria vault; other countries will have similar net yields around 7–10%.

All fees are deducted from the yield or deposit amount before you receive your share. There are no hidden charges.

Risks & Mitigations

As with any investment, risks exist. We address them directly:

  • Sovereign default – we diversify across six countries and will add more. Historical recovery rates for Eurobond defaults average 50–80% after restructuring. Your risk is identical to holding the bond directly, but spread across multiple issuers.
  • Smart contract vulnerability – only 10% of funds are onchain. Our contracts will be audited by CertiK or Trail of Bits. A bug bounty program and circuit breaker are planned.
  • Regulatory changes – we use licensed third party custodians and stay within existing frameworks. Our entity structure is designed to be adaptable.
  • Market liquidity – Eurobonds are traded on major exchanges. The 10% onchain reserve covers most redemptions. For larger exits, bond sales take 1–2 days – still far faster than traditional OTC markets.
