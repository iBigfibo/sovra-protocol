📘 Sovra Protocol – Documentation

How it works: Deposit USDC → 90% buys real sovereign Eurobonds (regulated custodian), 10% onchain reserve. Yield from bond coupons distributed in USDC after 0.1% deposit/withdrawal fees and a protocol fee (6.57% of gross yield). Net APY: ~8.41% (Nigeria vault).

Risks: Sovereign default, custodian risk, smart contract risk. See FAQ on main site.
