Frequently asked questions

What net APY can I expect? +
The Nigeria vault offers 8.41% after fees. Other countries (Angola, Turkey) will be similar – typically 7–9%.
How is yield generated? +
90% of each deposit buys real sovereign Eurobonds. The bond coupon income flows back to users after a small protocol fee.
Can I withdraw at any time? +
Yes. Small redemptions use the 10% onchain reserve instantly. Larger redemptions trigger a bond sale (1‑2 days). Withdrawal fee 0.1%.
Is there a minimum deposit? +
No minimum. You can deposit $1 or $1 million.
