Risks & transparency

What if a country defaults? +
Sovereign default is a real risk. Sovra diversifies across multiple markets. In a default, users may face delays or partial loss – identical to holding the bond directly.
Custodian failure? +
Bonds are held by regulated custodians with monthly audits. However, custodian insolvency remains an off-chain risk.
Smart contract audited? +
Audit with CertiK scheduled before mainnet. Oracle exploits mitigated by circuit breakers.
Why net APY 8.41% vs 10% gross? +
Only 90% deployed (10% reserve). After protocol fees, net = 8.41% – trade‑off for instant liquidity.
Can I lose everything? +
Yes – default, custodian hack, or smart contract bug could cause loss. We mitigate but risk remains.
